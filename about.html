About Andromeda Plumbing & Heating

Andromeda Plumbing & Heating is a locally owned company serving Edmonton and the surrounding area. The focus is simple: do clean work, show up when promised, and leave your home better than we found it.

Whether it’s a small leak, a full boiler and manifold system, or an emergency no-heat call, you’ll get clear communication and straightforward pricing.

  • Honest recommendations based on your situation
  • Respect for your time, budget and home
  • Quality materials that meet local code
