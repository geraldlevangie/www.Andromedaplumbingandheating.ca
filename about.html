About

Your local plumbing & heating partner

Andromeda Plumbing and Heating is a small, owner-operated company serving Edmonton, AB. The focus is simple: do the job right, be honest with people and stand behind the work.

Our approach

When you book a call with Andromeda, you’re dealing directly with the person who will show up at your door — not a call centre or a rotating cast of strangers. That means clear communication and accountability from start to finish.

We take the time to explain what’s going on, show you the options and answer your questions. You’ll know the price before work begins, and we’ll never pressure you into upgrades you don’t actually need.

  • Licensed and insured for plumbing and heating work.
  • Code-compliant installations with permits where required.
  • Respect for your home, flooring and personal space.

Why homeowners choose Andromeda

  • Straightforward advice. We’ll tell you if a repair makes sense or if it’s better to replace equipment.
  • Clean, organized work. Drop sheets, shoe covers and tidy work areas are standard.
  • Respect for your time. We provide realistic arrival windows and keep you updated if something changes.
  • Local knowledge. Experience with Edmonton’s housing stock, older neighbourhoods and winter conditions.

Serving Edmonton & nearby communities

Based in Edmonton, AB, Andromeda Plumbing and Heating works with homeowners, landlords and small businesses across the city and select surrounding areas. If you’re not sure whether you’re in the service area, just call and ask — we’re happy to let you know.
