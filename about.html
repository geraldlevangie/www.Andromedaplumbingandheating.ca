About Andromeda Plumbing & Heating

Andromeda Plumbing & Heating provides honest, professional plumbing and heating service for homeowners and small businesses in Edmonton and the surrounding area.

Every job is approached with careful planning, clean workmanship, and respect for your home or workplace. Whether it’s an emergency late-night call or a planned upgrade, the focus is on doing the work properly — not rushing through a quick patch.

From common repairs and fixture replacements to furnace issues and hot water tank installs, the goal is to provide reliable solutions that last.

What You Can Expect

  • Clear explanations of what needs to be done and why.
  • Fair, upfront pricing with no surprise add-ons.
  • Work that meets or exceeds local plumbing and heating codes.
  • Respectful service from the first call to the final cleanup.
